Chemistry Assignment

Chemistry is the branch of science that deals with the properties, composition, and structure of elements and compounds, how they can change, and the energy that is released or absorbed when they change.

Chemical Elements

The periodic table is a tabular array of the chemical elements organized by atomic number, from the element with the lowest atomic number, hydrogen, to the element with the highest atomic number, oganesson. The atomic number of an element is the number of protons in the nucleus of an atom of that element.

Chemical Equation

Chemical reactions are represented using chemical equations.Here's an example of chemical reaction:

2H2 + O2 = 2H2O

This quation represents the reaction between hydrogen gas (H2) and oxygen gas (O2) to form water (H2O) .

Important Concepts

Stoichiometry, by definition, is the calculation of the quantities of reactants or products in a chemical reaction using the relationships found in the balanced chemical equation.
